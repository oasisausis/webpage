# IITB Fresher Guide — Creative redesign

## Information architecture

The homepage now has six clear destinations:

1. Academics — merges `courses.html` and `grades.html`
2. Know your campus — merges `food.html` and `monsoon.html`
3. Meet your seniors
4. Clubs and student life
5. Getting to IIT Bombay
6. Emergency help

Legacy URLs redirect to their new merged sections, so existing bookmarks do not break.

## Upload

Upload the complete contents of this folder to the repository root, including the `assets` folder.

## Main design improvements

- Matte charcoal, sage, clay and muted lavender palette
- Editorial hero and image-led bento navigation
- Responsive sticky navigation
- Interactive tabs, filters, checklists and SPI calculator
- Searchable senior profiles with photos and conditional fields
- Reduced-motion support and keyboard focus states
- Local WebP images for faster loading
